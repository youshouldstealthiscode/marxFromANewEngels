# Unarmed African Americans Killed by Law Enforcement Officers in the United States

_This is a living document cataloging unarmed African Americans killed by law enforcement officers in the United States. It is sourced from the [Wikipedia article of the same name](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_unarmed_African_Americans_killed_by_law_enforcement_officers_in_the_United_States) and includes every entry documented there._

_The names listed here represent only a fraction of the total lives lost. Many cases go unreported, unrecorded, or uninvestigated. The vast majority of officers involved were never charged, convicted, or disciplined._

---

## 2026

- **Samuel Brown**, 56 — February 16, Woodlawn, Maryland. An officer struck him during a fight after finding him sleeping in a car at a stoplight, causing him to hit his head on the concrete; he died of his injuries on February 27.
- **Stephenson King**, 39 — March 11, Boston, Massachusetts. Shot and killed by police responding to a carjacking; the officer claimed King tried to run him over, but was charged with manslaughter after bodycam footage contradicted his account.
